ProblemYield liquidity needs exits and LP protection

Exit demand can become one-sided.

Early yield pools often need instant exits before liquidity is deep. When everyone sells the yield token into the base asset, LP inventory risk increases.

Risk should be priced where it appears. The pool can quote a higher fee when flow worsens exit pressure and a lower fee when flow restores balance.
